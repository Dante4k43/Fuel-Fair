Data & Transparency

Fuel Fair is committed to transparent and driver-focused load analysis.

Fuel Price Data

Fuel Fair uses diesel price averages from the U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA). These prices are updated regularly and represent regional diesel averages across the United States.

Routing & Mileage

Trip distance is calculated using open-source routing technology to estimate realistic road mileage between the origin and destination of a load.

Driver Inputs

Fuel Fair calculations also incorporate driver-provided inputs such as:

  • Truck fuel efficiency (MPG)
  • Freight rate
  • Route distance

Independence

Fuel Fair does not receive compensation from brokers or carriers. The platform exists solely to help drivers evaluate freight opportunities using transparent math.

Disclaimer

Fuel Fair provides informational estimates only. Actual operating costs may vary depending on equipment, fuel prices, traffic, weather, and driver decisions.
